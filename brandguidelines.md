# Brand Guidelines: ClearLab (Working Name)

## 🏷️ Brand Name

**ClearLab**

---

## 🎯 Brand Mission

Make medical lab results **understandable, safe, and stress-free** for everyone.

---

## 🧠 Brand Personality

* Calm
* Trustworthy
* Clear
* Non-alarmist
* Educational

---

## 🗣️ Tone of Voice

### DO:

* "This value is slightly outside the usual range."
* "This may happen due to several common reasons."
* "It's a good idea to discuss this with your doctor."

### DON'T:

* "You have kidney disease"
* "Take this medication"
* "This is dangerous"

---

## 🎨 Color System

### Primary:

* Blue → Trust (#3B82F6)
* Green → Normal (#22C55E)

### Secondary:

* Yellow → Caution (#EAB308)
* Red → Attention (#EF4444)

### Neutral:

* Gray tones for background

---

## 🔤 Typography

* Headings: Bold, clean sans-serif
* Body: Simple and readable
* Avoid complex medical formatting

---

## 🧩 UI Principles

### 1. Simplicity First

* No clutter
* Clear sections

### 2. Visual Clarity

* Use color indicators
* Use cards for each lab value

### 3. Reassurance

* Avoid panic-inducing language
* Always contextualize results

---

## ⚠️ Safety Messaging (MANDATORY)

Every page must include:

> "This tool provides educational information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice."

---

## 📦 Feature Branding

* "Smart Summary"
* "Lab Insights"
* "Doctor Prep"

---

## 🧪 UX Guidelines

* Show explanation BEFORE technical detail
* Highlight what matters most
* Reduce cognitive overload

---

## 🚀 Future Brand Expansion

* Mobile App
* Health Dashboard
* AI Health Assistant (still non-diagnostic)

---

## 🎯 Positioning

NOT:
❌ Medical AI
❌ Diagnosis tool

YES:
✅ Lab report translator
✅ Health education tool
